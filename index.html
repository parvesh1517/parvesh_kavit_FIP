Our Story


Welcome to Squeeze It, where our journey began with a simple passion for revitalizing the way people experience juice. Our story started in the heart of vibrant orchards, where the allure of fresh fruits and the desire for healthier beverage options collided. As we embarked on this journey, we realized that the traditional juice market was ripe for innovation. Armed with a dedication to quality and a thirst for creativity, we set out to redefine what it means to enjoy a refreshing drink.

After years of meticulous research and development, we proudly unveiled a lineup of revitalized juice drinks that encapsulate the essence of nature's goodness. Each bottle is a testament to our unwavering commitment to using only the finest, handpicked fruits, sourced from trusted growers who share our passion for excellence.
