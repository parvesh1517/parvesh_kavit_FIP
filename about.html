Our Purpose


At Squeeze it, we're here to revolutionize juice drinks with fresh flavors, quality ingredients, and a commitment to sustainability. Our mission is simple: to refresh and revitalize, while leaving a positive impact on the planet. Join us as we squeeze every drop of goodness out of life, one delicious sip at a time.

Furthermore, our commitment to sustainability extends beyond just our ingredients. From our packaging to our production processes, we continuously seek out innovative ways to minimize our environmental footprint. Whether it's utilizing eco-friendly packaging materials. implementing energy-efficient manufacturing practices, or actively reducing food waste, we're dedicated to making every aspect of our operations as planet-friendly as possible

At Squeeze It, we believe that by making conscious choices every day, we can create a brighter, more sustainable future for generations to come. Join us in our journey towards a healthier planet, one sip at a time.

Our Vision


At Squeeze It, our ambition is to be the top choice for delicious, high-quality juice drinks that are good for you and the planet. We aim to expand globally, innovate continuously, and lead the way in sustainability. Join us as we empower individuals to make healthier, more sustainable choices while enjoying every sip.

Moreover, our sustainability mission doesn't stop at the factory gates. We actively engage in initiatives to combat food waste, recognizing the pivotal role this plays in preserving our planet's resources. From optimizing distribution channels to implementing comprehensive waste reduction strategies, we're unwavering in our resolve to minimize environmental harm at every turn. Each decision we make is a conscious step towards a more sustainable future, reflecting our unwavering commitment to nurturing both our business and the planet we call home.
